## [1.0.0] - 2021-10-25

### Added

- Support for RPC commands:
    - create_free_account,
    - find_accounts,
    - get_account,
    - get_gateways,
    - get_gateway_fee,
    - get_nodes,
    - get_timestamp,
    - send_transaction.
- Support for transactions:
    - broadcast,
    - change_account_key
    - create_account
    - send_one.
- Blockchain crypto
- ADS utils

- Contributing
